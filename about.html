Who We Are

Nyakabirizi Miracle Centre Church is found in the heart of Uganda and God has cling the unique Mission of Extending Good Tidings to the meek and restoring the broken hearted upon His Servants in this place.

Our Mission

To proclaim the Good Tidings with power, nurture believers through the Word of God, and impact our community with love, Honor and truth.
