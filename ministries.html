Our Ministries

Galatians 2:20 – "The life I now live in the flesh I live by faith in the Son of God."

Youth Ministry

Our youth ministry is a place to grow in faith, friendship, and purpose. Every meeting is a chance to discover your gifts, share your story, and make an eternal impact.

Children's Ministry

Raising children in the fear of the Lord with fun, safe, and exciting activities that help every child grow in faith, kindness, and confidence.

Women's Ministry

We unite to grow spiritually, support each other, and strengthen our families. Equip women to fulfill their God-given purpose and shine God’s love in every area of life.

Men's Ministry

Building God-fearing men of faith and character, empowered to serve God and the community with integrity and courage.

Worship Team / Choir

Worship is a sacred encounter with God. Join us to experience praise, music, and spiritual growth in a dynamic worship atmosphere.

Outreach & Evangelism

Taking the Gospel beyond church walls, impacting our community through love, service, and practical action.
